Turn your Android device into a smart, automated surveillance system that puts you in control.

Welcome to Security Camera, the application that transforms your phone into an intelligent and reliable security solution. Our core mission is to provide peace of mind through powerful, easy-to-use features that prioritize your privacy and control.

Smart Motion Detection & Automated Recording

The app's intelligent algorithm instantly triggers high-quality video recording the moment motion is detected. You no longer need to sift through hours of footage; Security Camera captures only the moments that matter, ensuring you never miss an important event.

You are always in full command of your data. By default, all recordings are stored securely and locally on your device's internal memory or SD card. We believe your data is yours alone.

Secure Cloud Sync & Uninterrupted Background Operation

For enhanced accessibility, you can enable our secure Google Drive integration. Using a privacy-focused permission, the app will automatically upload your recordings to a dedicated folder in your personal Drive, making your footage accessible from anywhere without ever giving the app access to your other files.

Security Camera is built for dependability, with the ability to run seamlessly in the background or even with the screen turned off, all while keeping you informed with a persistent status notification.

With customizable motion sensitivity, powerful background monitoring, and a commitment to your privacy, Security Camera provides a robust security tool that is both effective and trustworthy.
